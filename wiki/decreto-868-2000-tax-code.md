# Decreto 868 (2000) --- Tax Code

## Summary
Decree No. 868 of 2000, officially the Law on Purchases and Contracts of the Public Administration (*Ley de Compras y Contrataciones de la Administración Pública*), served as El Salvador’s primary legal framework for public procurement for over two decades. The legislation established competitive bidding requirements, created institutional purchasing committees, and mandated transparency standards for government contracts involving goods, services, and public works. It was fully repealed by Legislative Decree No. 652, the Public Procurement Law, published in the Official Gazette on March 2, 2023.
