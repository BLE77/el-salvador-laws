# Trademark Registration and Protection in El Salvador

Trademark protection in El Salvador is governed by the **Ley de Marcas y Otros Signos Distintivos** and administered by the **Centro Nacional de Registros (CNR)**, specifically the Registro de la Propiedad Intelectual.

## Registration Process

To register a trademark in El Salvador:

1. **File an application** at the CNR with: the trademark name or design, a description of the goods or services, the applicable international class(es) under the Nice Classification, and a sample or reproduction of the mark
2. The CNR conducts a **formal examination** to verify the application meets requirements
3. The application is **published in the Diario Oficial** for an opposition period, during which third parties may challenge the registration
4. If no opposition is filed (or opposition is resolved favorably), the CNR issues the **registration certificate**

## Duration and Renewal

Trademark registration provides protection for **10 years** from the date of registration. It is **renewable indefinitely** for successive 10-year periods by filing a renewal application and paying the corresponding fee before expiration.

## International Protection

El Salvador is a member of the **Madrid Protocol**, allowing trademark owners to seek international registration through the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) system. A single application can extend protection to multiple member countries.

## Infringement and Remedies

Trademark owners can pursue both **civil and criminal remedies** against infringement:

- **Civil action**: Seek injunctions, damages, and destruction of infringing goods through commercial courts
- **Criminal action**: Trademark counterfeiting is a criminal offense under the Penal Code, carrying fines and imprisonment

## Practical Notes

Use of a trademark in commerce is not required for registration, but non-use for three consecutive years may expose the mark to cancellation. Engaging a local intellectual property attorney is recommended for navigating the registration process at CNR.
