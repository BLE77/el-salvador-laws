# Disability Rights in El Salvador

Disability rights in El Salvador are protected by the **Ley de Equiparacion de Oportunidades para las Personas con Discapacidad (Decreto 888 of 2000)** and its subsequent reforms. The law establishes a framework for equal opportunity and non-discrimination.

## Equal Access Rights

Persons with disabilities have the legal right to:

- **Equal access to education**: Public and private schools must provide accommodations and cannot deny enrollment based on disability
- **Equal access to healthcare**: The public health system must provide rehabilitation services and assistive devices
- **Access to public spaces and services**: Government offices, courts, and public institutions must be accessible

## Employment Protections

- Employers must provide **reasonable accommodation** (ajustes razonables) in the workplace for employees with disabilities
- Discrimination in hiring, promotion, or termination based on disability is prohibited
- **Tax benefits** are available to employers who hire workers with disabilities, including deductions on income tax
- The government promotes employment programs specifically for persons with disabilities through the Ministry of Labor

## Accessibility Requirements

- **Public buildings** must comply with accessibility standards, including ramps, elevators, accessible restrooms, and signage
- New construction of public facilities must incorporate universal design principles
- Public transportation systems are required to progressively implement accessibility features

## Social Security Disability Pension

Workers who become permanently disabled may qualify for a **disability pension** through the social security system (ISSS) or their pension fund (AFP). Requirements include medical certification of the disability and a minimum contribution period. The pension amount depends on the degree of disability and the worker's contribution history.

## Oversight

The **Consejo Nacional de Atencion Integral a la Persona con Discapacidad (CONAIPD)** oversees disability policy and receives complaints about rights violations.
