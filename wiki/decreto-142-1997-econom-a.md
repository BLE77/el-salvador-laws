# Decreto 142 (1997) --- Economía

## Summary
Decree No. 142 of 1997 establishes the comprehensive legal framework for regulating El Salvador's telecommunications sector, with particular emphasis on public telephone services. It empowers the State to supervise service provision, approve tariffs, manage the radio spectrum, and administer numbering plans while guaranteeing consumer protection and legal certainty for providers through principles of free competition and elimination of administrative arbitrariness.

## Key Provisions
- **Article 1**: Defines the object of the decree as the regulation of telecommunications activities, specifically the public telephone service, exploitation of the radioelectric spectrum, access to essential resources, and the national numbering plan including multi-system access codes.
- **Article 2**: Establishes the constitutional authority derived from Article 110 of the Constitution, which mandates the State to regulate and supervise public services and approve their respective tariffs.
