# Religious Freedom in El Salvador

Religious freedom is a constitutionally guaranteed right in El Salvador. **Article 25 of the Constitution** ensures the free exercise of all religions, and **Article 3** prohibits discrimination on religious grounds.

## Constitutional Protections

- **Free exercise of religion** — every person may freely practice their religion without restriction, provided it does not disturb public order or violate the rights of others
- **No state religion** — El Salvador does not have an official state religion. While the country is historically and predominantly Catholic, the government does not endorse or favor any particular denomination
- **Non-discrimination** — no person may be discriminated against based on their religious beliefs in employment, education, or public services

## Religious Organizations

Religious organizations can register as **non-profit legal entities** (personas juridicas sin fines de lucro) through the **Ministry of Interior (Ministerio de Gobernacion)**. Registration provides:

- **Legal personality** — the ability to own property, enter contracts, and operate officially
- **Tax exemptions** — registered religious organizations are exempt from income tax on activities related to their religious mission
- **Import benefits** — certain goods imported for religious purposes may receive duty exemptions

Foreign religious organizations operating in El Salvador must also register with the Ministry of Interior.

## Religious Education

Religious instruction is **not mandatory** in public schools. Parents have the right to choose the religious education of their children. Private religious schools may include religious instruction as part of their curriculum, but attendance at such schools is voluntary.

## Practical Notes

El Salvador's religious landscape includes a significant and growing Evangelical Protestant population alongside the Catholic majority. Relations between religious groups are generally peaceful, and interreligious dialogue is common in civic spaces.
