# Secondary Sources

## High-value backfill sources

### WIPO Lex

- [WIPO Lex El Salvador profile](https://www.wipo.int/wipolex/en/legislation/members/profile/SV)
- Best for intellectual property laws, regulations, treaties, and cleaner metadata around versions.

### ILO NATLEX

- [NATLEX](https://natlex.ilo.org/)
- Best for labor, employment, social security, workplace safety, and amendment chains.

### FAOLEX

- [FAOLEX El Salvador profile](https://www.fao.org/faolex/country-profiles/general-profile/en/?iso3=SLV)
- Best for agriculture, food, fisheries, forestry, land, water, and environmental legislation.

### ECOLEX

- [ECOLEX](https://www.ecolex.org/)
- Best for environmental, biodiversity, treaty, and natural-resource materials.

### UNHCR Refworld

- [Refworld legal instruments](https://www.refworld.org/legal/legislation/natlegbod/)
- Best for human-rights-adjacent laws, migration, asylum context, and translated or mirrored documents.

### UNODC SHERLOC

- [SHERLOC legislation database](https://sherloc.unodc.org/cld/v3/sherloc/legdb/index.html?lng=en)
- Best for organized crime, corruption, narcotics, trafficking, cybercrime, and security-related materials.

## Discovery and verification sources

### Library of Congress

- [Foreign Legal Gazettes collection](https://www.loc.gov/collections/foreign-legal-gazettes/about-this-collection/)
- [Guide to Law Online: El Salvador](https://guides.loc.gov/law-el-salvador/legal-guides)

Use it for:

- Gazette backfill
- Historical verification
- Discovery of free official and non-official links

### WorldLII

- [El Salvador legislation catalog](https://www.worldlii.org/catalog/50603.html)

Use it for:

- Legacy discovery
- Mapping older legislation to primary source URLs

### vLex

- [vLex El Salvador](https://sv.vlex.com/)

Use it for:

- Broad legal coverage if a paid license is available
- Fast backfill when the public web is fragmented

### ICNL

- [ICNL digital library, El Salvador filter](https://www.icnl.org/resources/library?_country_ol=el-salvador)

Use it for:

- Civil society, elections, transparency, NGO, and governance materials

### Constitute

- [Constitute Project: El Salvador](https://www.constituteproject.org/constitution/El_Salvador_2014)

Use it for:

- Constitution-only English access
- Comparative or bilingual AI retrieval

## Discovery-only references

- [GlobaLex: El Salvador](https://www.nyulawglobal.org/globalex/el_salvador1.html)
- [Lexadin / World Law Guide](http://www.lexadin.nl/wlg/legis/nofr/oeur/lxweesa.htm)
- [Foreign Law Guide](https://referenceworks.brillonline.com/browse/foreign-law-guide)

These are useful for finding sources and bibliographic leads, but they should not be treated as authoritative corpus records.
